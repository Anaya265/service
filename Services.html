Frequently Asked Questions



How can I hire a freelance web developer? ▼

You can contact me directly via the 'Contact Us' section or visit my Upwork/Fiverr profile to initiate a project.

What is the process of working with a freelance web developer?▼

First, we understand your requirements, then proceed to planning, followed by design and development phases. After delivery, revisions and feedback are also considered.

How do you estimate the project cost? ▼

The cost depends on the complexity, features, and timeline of the project. An accurate estimate is given after a detailed discussion.

What is the delivery time?▼

The delivery time depends on the project’s scope and complexity. Smaller projects usually take a few days, while larger projects may take more time.

How can I manage my website after development? ▼

After completion, I provide an easy-to-understand guide and support so that you can manage your website on your own.

Will my website be mobile-friendly? ▼

Yes, I design every website to be responsive, ensuring it works perfectly on all devices, whether mobile or desktop.

What if I want to make changes to the project? ▼

You can always contact me during the project for changes. Initial revisions are usually free, but major changes may incur additional charges.

Do you offer SEO services?▼

Yes, I provide basic on-page SEO services to help your website rank in search engines. Advanced SEO services can be discussed separately.

Can you help with website hosting and domain registration?▼

Yes, I can assist with website hosting and domain registration to ensure your website goes online smoothly.

How do support and maintenance work?▼

Even after project completion, I provide support and maintenance services to keep your website up-to-date and running smoothly.

Do you develop e-commerce websites?▼

Yes, I develop e-commerce websites with secure payment gateways and a user-friendly shopping experience.
